GG Gaminggg

How we got the name: Hey guys! This is about my Youtube channel. (Make sure you subscribe!) You probably want to know where the name "GG Gaminggg" came from: My sister and I were on a long drive to Naples, so we started a channel! We were kinda in the middle of nowhere, when we decided to start recording and play a game where you must answer a riddle by nature around you. A huge truck passed us and on the side it said "GG" so of course I called out "GG" and surly that was right. Well, we were playing a game and repeatedly yelling "gg" so we called our channel GG Gaminggg!
